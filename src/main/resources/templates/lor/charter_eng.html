1️⃣ Introduction to the World of KOMIKA

Once, the multiverse was an unstable chaos—an endless stream of energy and ideas without form. But everything changed when the Ancient Architects discovered the primal substance — KOMIKA.

  • 🔹 KOMIKA is not just matter; it is the source of all worlds.

This living crystal shapes reality, creates universes, and connects dimensions. Its internal iridescence resembles an ever-changing pattern of stories, plots, and destinies.

But Komika is not infinite. It forms only where the edges of universes intersect, where magic meets science, and swords clash with lasers.
Komika is more than just energy—it has become the rarest and most valuable substance in all worlds.

2️⃣ The Battle for Komika: Who Controls the Fate of the Universe?

KOMIKA has given powerful beings the tool to create, change, and destroy worlds, but it has also spawned an endless struggle for control.
Now, five factions not only exist—they fight for power!
Each faction strives to secure key positions in governing the multiverse:

  • ✅ Secure a seat in the High Council of Creators — 5 positions that allow one to control the fate of the multiverse.
  • ✅ Become a Judge of Reality — 7 seats that can veto the Council's decisions.
  • ✅ Gain control over the extraction of Komika to amplify their influence.

The factions not only oppose one another but also engage in complex political maneuvers, forming alliances and betraying allies.

🔹 The High Council of Creators

  • 📌 The primary stewards of KOMIKA's destiny, deciding which worlds are to be created, which dimensions are linked, and which are sealed forever.
  • 📌 5 seats on the Council — each faction aims to occupy them to impose its own philosophy on the multiverse.
  • 📌 A Creator's vote depends on their influence within the DAO (the higher the rating, the stronger the vote).

🔹 Judges of Reality

  • 📌 7 Judges who can veto the decisions of the Creators and maintain balance.
  • 📌 If 4 out of the 7 Judges vote against a decision, it is overturned.
  • 📌 Factions wage both covert and open wars to secure their seats among the Judges.

🔹 The Economy of Komika and the Struggle for Resource Control

  • 📌 Extraction of Komika is the key to power—the more a faction controls, the greater its influence.
  • 📌 Factions can restrict access to resources, monopolize extraction, or influence the economy.
  • 📌 Both peaceful and military conflicts arise over control of Komika deposits.

6️⃣ How is the Universe Governed? (Voting in the DAO)

🔹 The High Council of Creators

  • 📌 Each participant votes with their Komika tokens, allocating them among candidates for the Council.
  • 📌 The top 5 candidates by rating secure seats on the Council.
  • 📌 The higher a Creator's rating, the stronger their vote when decisions are made.

📌 The Council of Creators establishes the fundamental laws of the multiverse:

  • Which worlds will be created.
  • Which technologies or forms of magic are permitted.
  • Which resources may be extracted.

Factions strive to advance their candidates in the Council through campaigns, deals, alliances, and even sabotage.

🔹 Judges of Reality

  • 📌 7 Judges elected by the participants.
  • 📌 If 4 out of 7 Judges vote against a decision by the Council of Creators, it is blocked.
  • 📌 The Judges ensure balance, preventing destructive or unfair decisions.

Factions strive to control the Judges, as they determine the fate of key laws.

🔹 Direct Voting by the Seekers of Worlds

  • 📌 Any participant can vote directly on a proposed decision.
  • 📌 If direct voting achieves 52% relative to the rating of the High Council, the decision is accepted even without the Council's approval.
  • 📌 This empowers ordinary participants to influence the multiverse.

7️⃣ The Economy and Extraction of Komika

🔹 The extraction system employs a hybrid consensus:

  • ✅ The Strength of the Seekers (Proof‑of‑Work) – traditional mining by creating new realities and events.
  • ✅ The Energy of the Creators (Proof‑of‑Stake) – the more Komika you hold, the more influence points you earn.

🔹 Formation of Influence Points

  • 📌 Complexity Points – the more intricate a created world, the more energy it generates.
  • 📌 Staking Points – the longer Komika is held, the greater its power.
  • 📌 Transaction Points – active users receive more Komika.

Factions can influence the economy, regulate access to Komika, and form alliances for extraction.
